About Us

Being one of the leading B2B Online Travel Service providers

T3 offers affordable, instant, innovative & responsive travel services along with the best deals. Established in 2012, T3 intends to make your bookings & service delivery absolutely hassle-free.

We are committed to delivering the highest level of quality services. Our experienced management has a detailed analysis of the market and offers the following an understanding of the core travel management industry.
